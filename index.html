About Me

My name is Temon Barsoum. I'm a photographer and videographer based in the Seattle area with a passion for capturing moments that tell a love for motion, I turn ordinary scenes in to unforgettable visuals. Specializing in cinematic car videos and dynamic sports content, I focus on creating high-energy, visually striking work.


I specialize in creating high-quality videos for car owners, upcoming business owners and artists, helping them reach a wider audience and attract more clients. By showcasing their work, we not only support their growth but also expand TBshots network and business opportunities. My goal is to build lasting connections, increase exposure, and create valuable collaborations that benefit all parties in the long run!
